About Me

I am a fun, life-loving mom and wife. I love to spend my free time adventuring with my two little girls and husband. We love to travel, and I plan to put as many checks on my bucket list as I can! I am as girly as it gets and love all things pink or sparkly. When I am not traveling, I spend my time either doing aerial fitness, or coding. I started at a young age doing circus classes (trapeze, ballet, gymnastics, lyra- you name it!), and continue to take aerial classes (mostly pole and silks these days).

I started working towards a career in development because it so greatly combines my strengths and niches. I am able to be incredibly creative and showcase my keen eye for style and design. Technology is ever changing and there is no limit to where it may take us, so I plan to learn all that I can and explore it to the fullest!!
